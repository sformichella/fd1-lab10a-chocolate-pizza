Chocolate Pizza

Posted on 15 Dec 2013 / Desserts

For the fig-swirl: Melt butter over medium heat in a saucepan. Add brown sugar and stir to dissolve. Have All of the figs and toss in the saucepan with water and lemon juice. Cook over medium heat, stirring frequently, until you have a chunky-jammy mixtue. Add salt with one or two stirs, set aside and let cool completely.

Ice cream: In a small pot over medium heat, combine milk and granulated sugar until sugar is completely dissolved and the milk is just barely lukewarm. Whisk in the egg yolks. Set mixture in the fridge and wait untill the fig mixture in cooled.

Using an ice cream machine, pour liquids into the frozen basin and process according to the manufacturer instructions, i.e. let spin and thicken for 20 minutes before adding mascrarpone, fig jam mixture, and the nuts. Continue to process fpr +/- 10 minutes. Pour semi-frozen mixture into a pyrex dish or glass tupperware. Freeze for at least two hours before serving.

  • 1 1/2 cups milk
  • 1/2 cups mascrarpone
  • 1/2 tsp pink salt
  • 1lb Black Mission Figs
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 2-4 tbsp water
  • 1 1/2 cups heavy cream
  • 1/3 granulated sugar
  • 2 egg yolks
  • lemon, juice
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 cup honey roaste pecans, roughly choopped
